🍜 Hakka Noodles

Home

Ingredients

  • 200g noodles (Hakka or thin noodles)
  • 1 cup cabbage, finely shredded
  • 1/2 cup carrot, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup capsicum (bell pepper), sliced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon chili sauce (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon vinegar
  • 2 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Black pepper to taste
  • Spring onions for garnish

Instructions

  1. Boil water in a large pot. Add a pinch of salt and cook the noodles until they are just tender (al dente).
  2. Drain the noodles and rinse them under cold water to stop cooking. Add a little oil to prevent sticking.
  3. Heat oil in a wok on high flame. Add chopped garlic and sauté for a few seconds.
  4. Add carrots, capsicum, and cabbage. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes on high heat.
  5. Add soy sauce, chili sauce, vinegar, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  6. Add the cooked noodles and toss everything together gently.
  7. Cook for another 1-2 minutes while stirring.
  8. Garnish with spring onions and serve hot.

Preparation Time: 25 mins

Difficulty: Easy
