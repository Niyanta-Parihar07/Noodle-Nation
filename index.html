About Noodle Nation

Noodle Nation is a simple and interactive recipe website designed to showcase a variety of delicious noodle dishes from around the world. It helps users explore different recipes with clear ingredients and step-by-step instructions.

The purpose of this website is to make cooking easy and enjoyable, especially for beginners. From quick meals like Maggi to flavorful dishes like Ramen and Pad Thai, users can find recipes suited to their taste and skill level.

Features: Recipe cards, search bar UI, responsive design.
