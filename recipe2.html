🍜 Maggi

Home

Ingredients

  • 1 packet Maggi noodles
  • 1½ cups water
  • 1 tastemaker (comes with packet)
  • 1 tablespoon oil or butter (optional)
  • 1/4 cup chopped vegetables (onion, tomato, peas - optional)
  • Green chili (optional)
  • Coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat a pan and add water. Bring it to a boil.
  2. If using vegetables, add chopped onions, tomatoes, peas, and green chili. Cook for 1–2 minutes.
  3. Add the Maggi noodles to the boiling water.
  4. Add the tastemaker and mix well.
  5. Cook for 2-3 minutes while stirring occasionally until noodles soften.
  6. If desired, add butter or oil for extra flavor.
  7. Cook until the water is mostly absorbed and noodles are soft.
  8. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot.

Preparation Time: 5 mins

Difficulty: Easy
