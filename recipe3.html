🍜 Ramen

Home

Ingredients

  • 200g ramen noodles
  • 2 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 boiled egg (halved)
  • 1/2 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1/4 cup sweet corn
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon chili paste (optional)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • Spring onions (chopped)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat a pan and prepare the broth by adding garlic and soy sauce. Let it simmer for a few minutes.
  2. Add mushrooms and corn to the broth and cook for 3–4 minutes.
  3. In a separate pot, boil water and cook the ramen noodles according to the package instructions.
  4. Drain the noodles and set aside.
  5. Add the cooked noodles into the hot broth and mix gently.
  6. Season with salt, pepper, and chili paste if desired.
  7. Pour the ramen into a bowl and place the boiled egg on top.
  8. Garnish with chopped spring onions and serve hot.

Preparation Time: 40 mins

Difficulty: Medium
