🍜 Pad Thai

Home

Ingredients

  • 200g rice noodles
  • 2 tablespoons tamarind paste
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup bean sprouts
  • 1/4 cup roasted peanuts (crushed)
  • 2 cloves garlic (chopped)
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • Spring onions for garnish
  • Lemon wedges (optional)

Instructions

  1. Soak the rice noodles in warm water for 15-20 minutes until soft, then drain.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and sauté chopped garlic until fragrant.
  3. Push garlic to one side, crack an egg into the pan, and scramble it.
  4. Add soaked noodles to the pan and mix well.
  5. Add tamarind paste, soy sauce, and sugar. Toss everything together.
  6. Add bean sprouts and cook for 2-3 minutes on medium heat.
  7. Mix well until noodles are evenly coated with sauce.
  8. Garnish with crushed peanuts, spring onions, and lemon juice. Serve hot.

Preparation Time: 35 mins

Difficulty: Hard
