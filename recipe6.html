🍜 Chow Mein

Home

Ingredients

  • 200g noodles
  • 1 cup chicken or mixed vegetables
  • 1/2 cup cabbage (shredded)
  • 1/2 cup capsicum (sliced)
  • 1/2 cup carrots (thinly sliced)
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon chili sauce (optional)
  • 2 cloves garlic (chopped)
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Spring onions for garnish

Instructions

  1. Boil noodles in salted water until just cooked. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a wok on high flame. Add chopped garlic and sauté briefly.
  3. Add chicken or vegetables and stir-fry for 3-4 minutes.
  4. Add cabbage, capsicum, and carrots. Cook on high heat for 2-3 minutes.
  5. Add soy sauce, chili sauce, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  6. Add the cooked noodles and toss everything together evenly.
  7. Cook for another 1-2 minutes while stirring continuously.
  8. Garnish with spring onions and serve hot.

Preparation Time: 25 mins

Difficulty: Easy
